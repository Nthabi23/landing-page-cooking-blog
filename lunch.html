Teriyaki Chicken Bowl

Teriyaki chicken is a classic of Japanese cuisine. According to tradition, any dish cooked with teriyaki sauce should be eaten with steamed rice and vegetables as a garnish. All parts of the chicken can be used to prepare this dish. However, I advise you to use the thighs because, like the thighs, they remain much juicier. The recipe is very simple and can be prepared in just a few steps. Try it and let yourself be won over by its genuine goodness.
